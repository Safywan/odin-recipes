Fried Rice

Description

A beautiful and easy fried rice recipe made with simple home ingredients. The use of wok is a must!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup long grain rice
  • 2 tsp vegetable oil
  • 2 whisked eggs
  • 1 carrot, peeled and grated
  • 2 shallots
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 250g chicken breast

Steps

  1. Cook the rice in a large saucepan of boiling water for 12 minutes or until tender. Drain and leave to cool.
  2. Heat oil in non-stick wok or large frying pan over medium heat. Add eggs. Swirl over base to form an omelette. Cook for 2 minutes or until set. Transfer to a chopping board. Set aside to cool slightly. Roll up and thickly slice.
  3. Add chicken to wok. Cook 4 minutes until light golden. Add carrot. Stir fry 1 minute. Add shallots, peas and rice. Cook, stirring, 3-4 minutes. Add egg and soy sauce. Stir until heated through. Sprinkle with sesame seeds and top with extra shallots. Serve immediately.
